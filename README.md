🌅 Gateway Morning Practice
> A free, interactive guided wellness tool based on the **Monroe Institute Gateway Intermediate Workbook** — no app download, no account, no cost. Just open the page and begin.
Live site: efficiencylabs.github.io/G8way
---
What Is This?
The Gateway Program was developed by the Monroe Institute of Applied Sciences in the 1970s as a structured method for exploring and expanding human consciousness. The program's foundational workbook teaches a series of mental exercises designed to help practitioners enter deep states of focused relaxation, access expanded awareness, balance physical and emotional energy, and establish positive life patterns.
This web app takes those exercises and turns them into a guided morning session you can complete in 10–45 minutes — structured, timed, and adapted to five different daily intentions.
---
How the App Works
When you open the page, you:
Choose your intention for the session
Set your duration (10, 20, 30, or 45 minutes)
Follow step-by-step guidance through each technique, with an optional countdown timer per step
Return and integrate — prompted to write down your experiences before closing
The routine is automatically trimmed or expanded to fit your chosen duration. Every session begins with the foundational Gateway practices and then branches into techniques specific to your chosen focus.

The Techniques
Every session is built from a structured sequence of Gateway exercises. Here is what each one involves:
🌬️ Resonant Breathing Exercise
A six-part breathing practice drawn from the workbook. Each inhale draws fresh energy up through the entire body into the head; each exhale releases stale energy through the soles of the feet. Over six breath cycles, fresh energy is progressively filled from head to toe — described in the workbook as "filling an inverted jar full of new, fresh energy in six steps."
📦 Security Repository Box
Before entering deeper states, all worries, distractions, and unresolved problems are mentally placed into a sealed box for the duration of the session. This mental decluttering allows the mind to engage without fragmentation. Problems can be retrieved and examined — with reduced emotional charge — at the end.
🔟 Reaching Focus 10
Focus 10 is the foundational altered state of the Gateway system: mind awake, body asleep. The practitioner counts down from 1 to 10, each number bringing deeper physical relaxation while maintaining clear mental awareness. This is the base state from which all other exercises operate.
⚡ Energy Bar Tool (EBT)
A visualised tool — typically imagined as a glowing bar or wand of brilliant white light — that the practitioner charges with mental intention. The EBT is used to direct healing and balancing energy to specific parts of the body or emotional system. With practice, activating it produces a tangible sensation of warmth or charge in the solar plexus.
🫀 Living Body Map (LBM)
A luminous silhouette of the physical body, visualised in Focus 10. The practitioner systematically shifts the silhouette through different colours — blue for the nervous system, red for circulation, orange for muscles and bones, yellow for organs and glands — scanning for dim or flickering areas that indicate imbalance, then directing healing energy to those areas via the EBT.
💜 Emotional Body Map (EBM)
A finer, thinner silhouette that exists behind the Living Body Map — representing the emotional energy body. Dim or dark spots indicate stored emotional tension or imbalance. The practitioner applies soothing green energy to balance and bright purple energy to recharge, then sweeps the EBT from head to foot to cleanse the entire emotional system.
🎈 Resonant Energy Balloon (REBAL)
A sphere of resonant energy built through a specific breathing pattern — energy swirling inward on the inhale, expanding outward and downward on the exhale. Over 8–10 breath cycles, this field grows into a bright, protective balloon surrounding the practitioner. Once formed, it serves as both a protective shield and a vehicle for entering expanded perceptual states. When the REBAL becomes very light, it can carry the practitioner upward into Focus 12 and beyond.
🌀 Focus 12 — Awareness Expansion
An expanded state beyond Focus 10, reached when the charged, alive quality of deeper awareness begins to emerge naturally. At Focus 12, perception extends beyond the physical location. The practitioner holds their intention — a question, goal, or desired insight — then releases it and simply receives what arises: images, words, sensations, or thought-forms.
🕐 Clock Orientation Exercise
A spatial awareness exercise performed in Focus 12. The practitioner imagines a clock face with consciousness at the centre, then sends awareness out to each clock position — 12, 3, 6, 9 — observing what is perceived at each direction. The clock is then rotated 90 degrees and the process repeated. This trains consciousness to move freely in all directions and is used as preparation for remote viewing.
✨ Desired Pattern Projection
From Focus 12, the practitioner carefully builds a clear mental and emotional experience of a desired life pattern, habit, or position — feeling it as already real. This thought-pattern is then released and moved strongly into all areas of expanded awareness. According to the workbook, the pattern begins to form in physical reality when it is constructive and beneficial to the total self.
⚖️ Energy Balance
A targeted clearing exercise: the practitioner uses the EBT to cleanse the LBM and EBM, then retrieves one actionable problem from the Security Repository Box and applies cool, soothing green energy to strip away its excess emotional charge — leaving only the clear, practical matter to be addressed.
🔁 Return & Integration
A counted return from altered states — 10 down to 1 — with each number bringing full waking awareness back. The session closes with a prompt to immediately record any impressions, images, or insights before they fade.
